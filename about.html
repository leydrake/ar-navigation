About Us We are a team of four Information Technology (IT) students from BulSU Bustos, united by our goal of creating an innovative solution to improve campus navigation.

OUR VISION

To revolutionize campus navigation by combining innovation, accessibility, and immersive AR experiences, ensuring that no one feels lost within the university grounds.

OUR MISSION

  • Create a user-friendly mobile app with AR guidance
  • Enhance accessibility and efficiency in navigating the BulSU Bustos campus
  • Apply cutting-edge computer vision and AR technologies to real-world challenges
